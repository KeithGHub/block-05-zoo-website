Meet Our Giraffes!

Frankie

Frankie can't get his day started on a good note until he's had his bowl of grass! He tends to share his food with his pal Coconut.

Coconut

Coconut likes to start her day with a bowl of water and a nice serving of grass to go with it! While Coconut enjoys Frankie sharing his food with her she does not like sharing her food with him.
